Welcome to Sorensen Photography

Our Photographer

Timothy James Sorensen has twelve years of experience as a staff photographer. Tim got his first camera, a Nikon , when he was eight years old. He fell in love with taking photographs and catching those once in a lifetime moments on film. He attended the University of Miami, where he was staff photographer for the student newspaper and contributed to many of the college's web pages. From his humble beginnings in studio work at a JC Penney Portrait studio to newspaper work at Creative Loafing and Tampa Bay Times Neighborhood News, Tim has honed his skills as a photographer and found the best backdrops Tampa Bay has to offer. In addition, Tim has 10 years experience in freelance work with family portraits, weddings, school portraits, and infant photography.

Our Studio

Sorensen Photography is a fully functional studio centrally located in downtown St. Petersburg on 1235 Central Avenue. The space is open and perfect for setting up any backdrop. We specialize in providing a unique, home-away-from-home experience for your comfort so that each session is a personalized photography session.

Our Staff

Owners, Timothy and Jenny Sorensen are the primary photographers. Each has a degree in photojournalism from the University of Miami, where they met and became lifelong partners. Also on staff is Tom Harrison, Photographer/Graphic Designer. Tom has a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Graphic Design from Ringling College of Art & Design. Our multi talented Studio Manager, Judith Burns has a degree in Art & Art History from the University of South Florida. We are also fortunate to have Carrie Rodriguez, Photography Intern from the University of South Florida.
